Our Returns Policy

Easy Returns

Not satisfied with your purchase? Return it within 7 days for a full refund or exchange. Some restrictions apply.

Quality Guarantee

We guarantee the quality of all our products. If you receive damaged or spoiled items, we'll replace them immediately.

No Questions Asked

For quality issues, we offer no-questions-asked returns. Just let us know and we'll take care of it.

How to Return an Item

Initiate Return

Log in to your account and go to "My Orders" to initiate a return request within 7 days of delivery.

Package Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all tags and labels intact.

Schedule Pickup

We'll arrange for a pickup at your convenience. For quality issues, pickup is free of charge.

Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 3-5 business days.

Detailed Returns Policy

Eligibility for Returns

Most items can be returned within 7 days of delivery, provided they are in their original condition with all packaging intact. Some items have different policies:

  • Perishable groceries: Can only be returned if spoiled or damaged upon delivery
  • Medicines: Non-returnable for safety reasons
  • Personal care items: Must be unopened and in original packaging

Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times vary by payment provider:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 3-5 business days
  • UPI: 1-2 business days
  • Net Banking: 3-7 business days
  • Wallet Payments: Instant to 24 hours

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned unless damaged or defective:

  • Fresh produce (fruits, vegetables)
  • Dairy products and eggs
  • Meat, poultry, and seafood
  • Prepared foods
  • Personal care and beauty products
  • Medicines and healthcare items

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged item or incorrect product:

  1. Contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  2. Provide photos of the damaged/incorrect item
  3. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund immediately

For perishable items, we may require the item to be returned for quality inspection.

Returns FAQs

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive your return, we typically process refunds within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution.

What if I receive a damaged item?

If you receive a damaged item, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund as per your preference, usually within 24 hours.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes, you can request an exchange for most items. Simply select the exchange option when initiating your return. Exchanges are subject to availability of the replacement item.

Who pays for return shipping?

For returns due to our error (wrong item, damaged item, etc.), we cover all return shipping costs. For other returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping unless you choose to drop off the item at one of our partner locations.

What if I miss the return window?

While we strictly adhere to our 7-day return policy, we may make exceptions in certain circumstances. Please contact our customer service team to discuss your situation.
