Delivery Options

Standard Delivery

Free delivery on orders over ₹500. Delivery within 2-3 business days. Orders placed before 12 PM will be processed the same day.

Express Delivery

Get your order within 24 hours for an additional ₹150. Available in select areas. Order before 3 PM for same-day delivery.

Scheduled Delivery

Choose your preferred delivery date and time slot. Available for all orders. Additional charges may apply for specific time slots.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can use this link to track your order in real-time and get estimated delivery times.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 8 AM to 9 PM daily, including weekends. For scheduled deliveries, you can choose your preferred time slot during checkout.

Do you deliver on holidays?

Yes, we deliver 365 days a year! However, delivery times may be slightly longer on major holidays.

What if I'm not home when delivery arrives?

Our delivery partners will attempt to contact you. If you're unavailable, perishable items will be returned and refunded, while non-perishable items will be left in a safe place if possible.

Can I change my delivery address after ordering?

Yes, you can change your delivery address within 1 hour of placing your order by contacting our customer service. After that, changes may not be possible as we process orders quickly.
