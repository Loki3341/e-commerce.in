Shipping Policy

Our Shipping Process

At LuxeMarket, we strive to deliver your premium products with care and efficiency. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping process:

Domestic Shipping (Within India)

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days - ₹99 (Free on orders over ₹5000)
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days - ₹199
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available in select metropolitan areas - ₹299 (Order before 12 PM)

International Shipping

  • Standard International: 7-14 business days - Shipping costs vary by destination
  • Express International: 3-7 business days - Shipping costs vary by destination

Please note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to circumstances beyond our control such as weather, customs delays, or carrier delays.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Order Processing Schedule

  • Orders placed before 2 PM IST are processed the same day
  • Orders placed after 2 PM IST are processed the next business day
  • Weekend orders are processed on the following Monday

Shipping Restrictions

We currently ship to most countries worldwide. However, there are some restrictions:

  • Certain luxury items may have shipping restrictions based on local laws
  • Some remote locations may have limited shipping options
  • We cannot ship to PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses

Customs & Import Taxes

For international orders, customers are responsible for any customs and import taxes that may apply. LuxeMarket is not responsible for delays due to customs.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package.

Tracking Your Order

  • Domestic orders: Trackable within 24 hours of shipment
  • International orders: Trackable once the package reaches the destination country
  • For any tracking issues, please contact our customer service

Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package is lost in transit or arrives damaged, please contact us immediately at support@luxemarket.com with your order number and photos of the damaged items.

Our Guarantee

  • Lost packages will be reshipped at no additional cost
  • Damaged items will be replaced or refunded
  • Claims must be made within 7 days of delivery

For returns and exchanges, please refer to our Return Policy.
