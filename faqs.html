Ordering & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. All transactions are securely processed through our encrypted payment gateway.

Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your personal information. We don't store your credit card details on our servers - all payments are processed through PCI-compliant payment processors.

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

You can request changes or cancellation within 1 hour of placing your order by contacting our customer service. After that, we process orders quickly to ensure fast delivery, so changes may not be possible.

Do you offer cash on delivery (COD)?

Yes, we offer COD for most locations in India. A small processing fee of ₹50 applies to COD orders. Some high-value items may require prepayment for security reasons.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within India takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping delivers in 1-2 business days. International shipping times vary by destination but typically take 7-14 business days.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. You'll see available options and costs at checkout based on your location.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

What should I do if my package is lost or stolen?

Contact us immediately if your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package. We'll work with the carrier to investigate and will reship or refund as appropriate.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns for most items in original condition. Some items like underwear and personalized products are final sale. Please see our full Returns Policy for details.

How do I return an item?

Log into your account to initiate a return, print the prepaid label (if eligible), and ship the item back to us. For defective items, we'll email you a prepaid return label.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. It may take additional time for the refund to appear in your account depending on your payment method.

Can I exchange an item for a different size/color?

Yes, you can request an exchange when initiating your return. If the item you want is available, we'll ship it once we receive your return. Exchanges typically ship within 2-3 business days.

Products & Quality

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely. We source all our products directly from authorized dealers and brands. Every item is 100% authentic and comes with all original packaging and documentation.

Do you offer warranties on your products?

Most products come with a manufacturer's warranty. The warranty period varies by brand and product. Please check individual product pages for warranty details or contact us for specific information.

Can I see more photos of a product?

We provide multiple high-resolution photos for each product. If you'd like to see additional angles or details, please contact our customer service and we'll be happy to assist.

How do I care for my LuxeMarket products?

Each product comes with care instructions. For specific care questions, please refer to the product documentation or contact our customer service team for guidance.

Still Have Questions?

Our customer service team is available 24/7 to help with any additional questions you may have.
