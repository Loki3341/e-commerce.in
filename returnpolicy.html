Returns & Exchanges

Our Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your LuxeMarket purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're happy to offer returns or exchanges within 30 days of delivery.

Extended Holiday Returns: Items purchased between November 1st and December 31st may be returned until January 31st of the following year.

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in original condition with all tags attached
  • Original proof of purchase is required
  • Certain items are final sale (see non-returnable items below)
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items: For hygiene reasons, underwear, swimwear, and earrings cannot be returned unless faulty. Personalized or made-to-order items are also final sale.

How to Return or Exchange

Follow these simple steps to return or exchange your LuxeMarket purchase:

1

Initiate Your Return

Log in to your account and go to "Order History" to start your return or contact our customer service.

2

Package Your Item

Include all original packaging, tags, and a copy of your invoice. Use the prepaid return label if applicable.

3

Ship It Back

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping center. We recommend using a trackable shipping method.

4

Receive Refund/Exchange

Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund or ship your exchange within 3-5 business days.

Return Shipping

  • Free Returns: We provide free return shipping for defective or incorrect items
  • Customer-Paid Returns: For change of mind returns, customer pays return shipping
  • International Returns: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs and any customs fees

Refund Information

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item.

Refund Processing

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method
  • Sale items are eligible for store credit only
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable

Exchange Processing

  • Exchanges are subject to product availability
  • If the item you want is not available, we will issue a refund
  • Price differences will be charged or refunded as applicable
  • Exchanges typically ship within 2-3 business days after we receive your return

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@luxemarket.com with your order number and photos of the damaged item.

Our Process for Damaged Items

  • We will email you a prepaid return shipping label
  • Return the damaged item using the provided label
  • We will ship a replacement as soon as we receive your return
  • If a replacement isn't available, we will issue a full refund

Note: Claims for damaged or defective items must be made within 7 days of delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process my return?

Returns are typically processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your package. Refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear in your account depending on your payment method.

Can I return an item I purchased on sale?

Sale items can be returned for store credit within 30 days of purchase. Original sale discounts will be honored if the same item is repurchased.

What if I received the wrong item?

If you received an incorrect item, please contact us immediately. We will provide a prepaid return label and expedite shipping of the correct item at no additional cost to you.

Can I return an item without the original packaging?

While we prefer items to be returned in their original packaging, we understand this isn't always possible. As long as the item is in new, unused condition with all tags attached, we can accept the return.
